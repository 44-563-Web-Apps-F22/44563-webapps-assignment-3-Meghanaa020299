Middletown Maltese

Maltese are affectionate toy dogs weighing less than seven pounds, covered by a long, straight, silky coat.Maltese are hardy and adaptable pets.Maltese are generally agreeable and can get along well with children, adults of all ages, and live well with cats and other dogs.
